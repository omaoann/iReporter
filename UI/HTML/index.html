Welcome to Ireporter

iReporter is an initiative that allows people from africa to bring to light corruption cases to the relevant authorities and to the general public.

Ireporter also enables users to report on things that need government intervention.

It also allows users to contribute pictures, video and geolocation of reported interventions and red flags raised.

It is similar to Wikinews in that it allows, and encourages, ordinary citizens to submit stories, photos and videos related to news of any sort.

This can range from breaking news to a story that a person believes is newsworthy.
